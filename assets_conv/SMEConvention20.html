About SME Con'20

SME NITK Convention'20 was fun-filled with events for 2 days. The chapter had organized different events for the students of mining engineering and allied branches from across the country.

More than 50 students from various colleges took part in these competitions and won exciting cash prizes. Students engaged in various informative lectures by industry experts from various fields.

With the events like Gold Seeker and Model Mine Visit, the students got the opportunity to explore the beautiful campus of NITK.

Having organized the 1st SME NITK Annual Convention successfully in March 2020,it is going for 2nd Annual Convention exclusively open for all Mining & Allied Engineering disciplines.

PAPER PRESENTATION

People from fields of Mining and Allied Engineering branches were invited to present their paper to a panel of judges by conveying their orignal and creative idea in a simplistic manner. The idea was to inculcate the knowledge of upcoming research to the attendees of the event.
